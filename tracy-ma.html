Tracy Ma

When: April 23rd, 2019 @ 5pm
Where: Johnson/Kaplan Building,
66 W 12th St. Room A404

Parsons CD Lecture Series: Tracy Ma

Tracy Ma is a Hong Kong-born Canadian graphic designer. Her work has been recognized by the Type Directors Club, the Society of Print Designers, D&AD, Print magazine's "20 under 30" and Complex magazine's "25 Young Designers to Watch". She has given several talks in cities across Canada, America and Europe about her work. She is currently a visual editor at The New York Times.
